Drought Relief in the Marathwada Region

12 May, 2016 - 11 August, 2016

Drought relief activities will be carried out by two implementing partners of HAI, namely SEEDS India and ASVSS in the districts of Solapur and Beeds, with three areas of focus, i.e. 1) provision of drinking water, 2) fodder for livestock, and 3) rejuvenation of wells and check dams. Your contribution is solicited to carry out much needed relief activities. Individual donors may earmark their contribution to a particular implementing partner and/or to any particular activity.
